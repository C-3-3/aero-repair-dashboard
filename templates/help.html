🛠 Dashboard Help & User Guide

This dashboard was built in-house to support Aero Repair Corp's Part 145 operations by improving visibility, accountability, and documentation across the facility.

🔧 Available Tools

  • 🧰 Maintenance Tasks: Create, assign, and track internal jobs for facilities, tooling, or production support. Role-based permissions apply — technicians can view and complete, managers can create and delete.
  • ✍️ Digital Sign-Off Log: Submit and view technician sign-offs for completed work. Each entry is timestamped and linked to the task or service performed.
  • 🔍 Part Inspections: Document the condition of received parts, including packaging, paperwork, and general acceptance notes.
  • ⏰ Expiry Tracker: Monitor important expiration dates like calibration, training, and regulatory documents. Alerts are triggered automatically for upcoming expirations.
  • 📁 PDF Log: Review the history of automatically sorted incoming PDFs. Files are routed based on keywords found in their content and filed accordingly.
